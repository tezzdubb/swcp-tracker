SWCP Tracker

Track your 630-mile journey along the South West Coast Path — 52 sections, an interactive map, and a walking journal.

Free · private · no accounts · no ads

▶ Google Play — coming soon ☕ Support on Ko-fi

Everything you need to log the walk

📈

Progress at a glance

A progress ring with miles walked, miles remaining and sections done — plus your recent completions.

✅

Whole or partial walks

Tick off full sections, or log stretch-by-stretch between villages — finish every stretch and the section completes itself.

🗺️

Offline-friendly map

The entire trail over outdoor maps: walked in green, remaining in grey. Areas you've viewed keep working without signal.

📓

Walking journal

Route notes and difficulty for every section, a date-walked picker, and timestamped journal entries with photos.

💾

Backup & restore

Export everything to a single file. Restore it on a new phone whenever you like.

🥾

Made for the path

All 52 official sections from Minehead to South Haven Point, with distances in miles and kilometres.

🔒

Private by design

No accounts, no ads, no analytics and no tracking. Your walking history, notes and photos never leave your phone unless you export them yourself. Read the privacy policy — it's refreshingly short.

Support the project

The app is free and will stay free. If it's been useful on your walk, you can buy me a coffee — and if you love the path itself, consider supporting the charity that keeps the trail walkable.

SWCP Tracker is an unofficial, independent app. It is not affiliated with or endorsed by the South West Coast Path Association or National Trails. It's a progress log, not a navigation tool — always carry a proper map and check tides, ferries and firing-range times.
