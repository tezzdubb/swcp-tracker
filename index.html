SWCP Tracker

Track your progress along the 630-mile South West Coast Path — 52 sections, an interactive map, and a walking journal. Free, private, no accounts.

Get it on Google Play (coming soon) ☕ Support on Ko-fi

What it does

  • Progress at a glance — a progress ring with miles walked, miles remaining and sections completed.
  • All 52 official sections — tick them off whole, or log partial walks stretch-by-stretch between villages.
  • Interactive map — the entire trail drawn over outdoor maps, walked stretches in green, remaining in grey. Viewed areas work offline on the trail.
  • Walking journal — dated notes and photos for every section.
  • Backup & restore — export everything to a single file; move phones whenever you like.

Private by design

There are no accounts, no ads, no analytics and no tracking. Your walking history, notes and photos never leave your phone unless you export them yourself. See the privacy policy.

Support the project

The app is free and will stay free. If it's been useful on your walk, you can buy me a coffee on Ko-fi — and if you love the path itself, consider supporting the South West Coast Path Association, the charity that keeps the trail walkable.

SWCP Tracker is an unofficial, independent app. It is not affiliated with or endorsed by the South West Coast Path Association or National Trails. It's a progress log, not a navigation tool — always carry a proper map and check tides, ferries and firing-range times.
